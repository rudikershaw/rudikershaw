My Bibliography

I enjoy a good read, and a few years or so back I started keeping track of most of what I read, when I finished reading it, and what I thought of it. I decided I ought to put this information somewhere more accessible, and what better place than on my website? A lot of these books have shaped the way I think about the world. So, if you want to know a little bit about how I think you can start at the bottom and work your way through.
